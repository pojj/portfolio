A bit about me

I'm a Computer Science student at the University of British Columbia who loves turning ambiguous ideas into products people actually use. My work lately lives at the intersection of AI and practical engineering — designing copilots and agentic systems, then wiring them into reliable, full-stack infrastructure.

Across internships at Skydio, HeyGen, and Cyberium, I've built natural-language copilots for drone operations, an AI email-to-video pipeline, usage-metered billing driving real revenue, and backends engineered to scale under load. I care about clean abstractions, fast feedback loops, and shipping.

Outside of internships I build games, tools, and ML side projects — usually whatever I'm curious enough to take apart and rebuild myself.
